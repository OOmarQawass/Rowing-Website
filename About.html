About our club

The Nelson Rowing Club stands as a beacon of rowing excellence in the scenic region of Nelson, New Zealand. Founded over a century ago, the club has played a pivotal role in shaping the local rowing community and nurturing generations of talented athletes. Situated amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the Nelson region, the club offers its members unparalleled access to pristine waterways, including the picturesque Whakatu Inlet and the tranquil waters of the Maitai River, providing an idyllic setting for training and competition. At the heart of the Nelson Rowing Club's ethos lies a commitment to fostering both competitive excellence and a strong sense of camaraderie among its members. The club welcomes rowers of all ages and skill levels, from novice enthusiasts to seasoned competitors, providing comprehensive coaching programs and state-of-the-art facilities to support their development. With a rich history of success at regional and national regattas, the Nelson Rowing Club continues to uphold its legacy as a powerhouse in New Zealand rowing, consistently producing athletes who excel on both the domestic and international stages. Beyond its achievements in sport, the Nelson Rowing Club plays an integral role in the local community, serving as a hub for social gatherings, events, and recreational activities. Through its inclusive and welcoming atmosphere, the club fosters lifelong friendships and a deep appreciation for the sport of rowing. As it looks towards the future, the Nelson Rowing Club remains dedicated to inspiring the next generation of rowers, preserving its heritage, and continuing its legacy of excellence in New Zealand rowing.
